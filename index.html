Ikimono Website! Amazing!

Ikimono represents a call back to the reason that we all started; for fun. Many of us express ourselves through yoyoing, and so the yoyos we use should be just as intricate, special, and fun as we are. That’s why we want our yoyos to be an experience in every aspect, with absolutely no compromises and an extraordinary level of care and quality. We just want our friends to have good yoyos, yoyos they deserve. Lucky for us, we’re all friends here!
